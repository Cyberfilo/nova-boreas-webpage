STEM Racing is an educational program supported by Formula 1 that uses motorsport to make science, technology, engineering, and math more exciting. Students are challenged to design, build, and race miniature Formula 1 cars, turning classroom learning into a real-world experience.

Working in teams, students take on roles like engineers, designers, and project managers, just like in a real F1 team. They use professional software to create 3D models, test performance, and manufacture their cars before racing them on the track.

Beyond engineering, STEM Racing helps students develop teamwork, creativity, and communication skills. By mixing technical knowledge with business and presentation skills, the program prepares young people to solve problems and think innovatively about the future.

üéì Our School

BDC School of Milan is an accredited international bilingual school offering education from nursery through high school. Founded in 2013, the school is committed to preparing students for a globalized world through a balanced curriculum delivered in both Italian and English. Its educational philosophy focuses on academic excellence, personal development, and the cultivation of critical thinking, creativity, and global awareness.

With campuses in Milan and the surrounding Lombardy area, BDC School serves a diverse, international community. The school emphasizes an innovative and holistic approach to learning, integrating STEAM subjects, entrepreneurship, design thinking, and sports into the curriculum. This interdisciplinary model encourages experiential learning, collaboration, and problem-solving, helping students develop skills that extend beyond the classroom.

BDC School continues to evolve by introducing forward-thinking educational initiatives and internationally recognized programs. Through projects, challenges, and global academic pathways, the school aims to equip students with the knowledge, adaptability, and leadership skills needed to succeed in higher education and in an ever-changing professional landscape.
